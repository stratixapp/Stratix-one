1
Who We Are

Stratix One is a free business management app built for Indian MSME shop owners and traders — specifically kirana shops, mobile stores, cloth merchants, and daily traders in Tier 2/3 cities.

The app is developed and operated by the Stratix team. For questions, contact us at privacy@stratix.app.

2
What Data We Collect & Where It Lives
Data Type What It Includes Where Stored
Google Account Info Name, email, profile photo — received from Google when you sign in Device Only
Ledger Entries Income and expense amounts, notes, timestamps Device Only
Customer (Khata) Data Customer names, phone numbers, due amounts, transaction history Device Only
Bills Bill items, amounts, customer names, timestamps Device Only
Notes Text notes you write in the app Device Only
Cloud Sync Data All of the above, if you enable Cloud Sync Firebase (Encrypted)
Advertising / Analytics We do not collect any Never Collected
Location Data We do not collect any Never Collected

✅ By default, ALL your business data is stored only on your device using browser localStorage. It never leaves your phone unless you explicitly enable Cloud Sync.

3
How We Use Your Data
  • To authenticate you securely via Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication).
  • To display your name and business in the app.
  • To sync your business data across your devices only if you enable Cloud Sync.
  • To send daily business reminders if you enable notifications — this is device-only, no data is sent to a server.

✅ We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or profiling. Your data is never sold.

4
Third-Party Services

Stratix One uses these third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — Handles Google Sign-In securely. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Cloud Firestore (Google) — Stores your data in the cloud if you enable Cloud Sync. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. See Google Cloud Privacy.
  • Google Fonts — Loads fonts (Plus Jakarta Sans, DM Mono). No personal data is shared.
  • WhatsApp — Bills are shared through your device's WhatsApp app. We do not access or store your WhatsApp messages.
5
Data Security
  • Local data is stored in your device's browser storage — it is sandboxed and cannot be accessed by other apps.
  • Cloud-synced data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption) and stored in Google Firestore with strict security rules that allow only your account to access your data.
  • We do not store your data on our own servers.
  • Google Sign-In tokens are managed by Firebase and are never stored by us in plain text.
6
Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View — All your data is visible inside the app at any time.
  • Export — Export all data as JSON or CSV from Settings → Local Backup.
  • Delete Local Data — Go to Settings → Clear All Data to permanently delete everything on your device.
  • Delete Cloud Data — Contact us at privacy@stratix.app to request deletion of cloud-synced data.
  • Sign Out — Tap your profile icon → Sign Out to disconnect your Google account from the app.
7
Children's Privacy

Stratix One is a business app intended for adults and business owners. It is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. The "last updated" date at the bottom reflects the most recent revision.
