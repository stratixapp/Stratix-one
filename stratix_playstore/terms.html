Description of Service

Stratix One is a free business management tool designed for Indian MSME shop owners, kirana stores, and daily traders. The App provides:

  • Daily Ledger — Track daily income and expenses.
  • Khata Book — Manage customer dues and credit (udhaar).
  • WhatsApp Billing — Create and share bills with customers via WhatsApp.
  • Notes — Write and save business notes.
  • Cloud Sync — Optionally sync data across devices using Google Firebase.
  • Backup / Export — Export data as JSON or CSV.
